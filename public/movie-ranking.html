The Best Movies According To Chanakya

My Top 3 Movies of all Time

Jersey

Jersey is a Telugu-language sports drama film released in 2019. Directed by Gowtam Tinnanuri, it stars Nani in the lead role alongside Shraddha Srinath, with music composed by Anirudh Ravichander. The movie was widely acclaimed for its emotional depth, storytelling, and performances, particularly that of Nani

Good Will Hunting

Good Will Hunting is a critically acclaimed drama film released in 1997, directed by Gus Van Sant and written by Matt Damon and Ben Affleck, who also star in the movie. It's widely regarded as a cinematic masterpiece for its heartfelt storytelling, memorable performances, and deep exploration of themes like genius, trauma, and personal growth.

Interstellar

Interstellar is a 2014 epic science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan, known for his complex storytelling and visual brilliance. The movie features a star-studded cast including Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Chastain, and Michael Caine, with a stunning score by Hans Zimmer. It's a masterpiece that combines science, philosophy, and human emotion, exploring themes of love, survival, and the mysteries of the universe.
